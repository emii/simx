CD54

Other designations:

7F7 BB2 human rhinovirus receptor ICAM-1 [intercellular adhesion molecule 1] IFN-gamma regulated human melanoma-associated antigen Ly47 MALA-2 (mouse) Me14-D12 P3.58

The antigen is expressed on hematopoietic cells and non-hematopoietic cells.

The counterreceptor for CD54 is LFA-1 (CD11a) (Bella et al, 1998).

The lack of CD54 expression in knock-out mice has been shown to impair inflammatory and immune responses (Sligh et al, 1993).
